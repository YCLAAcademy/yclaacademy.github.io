Our mission is

To provide a free and quality school education for rural Tamil Nadu students and/or teachers using Tanglish (Tamil+English).

Teaching in Tamil with English terminology, so that both Tamil and English medium Students benefits. Our videos are useful for Students as well as Instructors. It is a sub-channel of YCLA Academy which aims for graduate-level computer Science (CS) whereas Teach For TN aimed at school level CS. #Teach4TN
